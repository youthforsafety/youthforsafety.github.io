Youth For Safety

A website anti-child sex abuse

 

The Youth for Safety Advocacy Campaign Project is an initiative to reduce , prevent , raise awareness and create more action against sexual child abuse. It primarily involves 100 youth advocates from 51 PCMN member organizations who will reach out to 7,500 children and youth who are at-risk to sexual abuse, exploitation and trafficking

Be part of Youth for safety's cause. VOLUNTEER and become a youth advocate. REPORT on cases of child sexual abuse. Make a DONATION so that we can protect and support more children who were sexually abused.

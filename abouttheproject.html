YOUTH FOR SAFETY

An advocacy campaign project of Philippine Children Ministries Network (PCMN) against child sexual abuse

Almost one of every two Filipinos lived in urban areas- with Metro Manila as the most populated urban area. Around 1.7 million children are living in informal settlements, working on the streets, which make them more vulnerable to human trafficking, abuse and exploitation.

Goal

Children and youth in Metro Manila, Visayas and Mindanao (and long term scale throughout the Philippines) live in safety and receive of their basic right to safety, due to a reduction of Child Sexual Abuse cases.

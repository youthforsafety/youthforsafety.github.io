About the Project

The Youth for Safety Advocacy Campaign Project is an initiative to reduce, prevent, raise awareness and create more action against sexual child abuse.

It primarily involves 100 youth advocates from 51 PCMN member organizations who will reach out to 7,500 children and youth who are at-risk to sexual abuse, exploitation and trafficking.

Goal

Children and youth in Metro Manila, Visayas and Mindanao (and long term scale throughout the Philippines) live in safety and receive of their basic right to safety, due to a reduction of Child Sexual Abuse cases.
